Frequently Asked Questions

How does delivery work?

We deliver orders within 30 minutes from your nearest FreshCart store. Delivery is available 7 days a week from 8 AM to 10 PM.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and UPI payments. You can also pay cash on delivery in select areas.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes! If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange items within 24 hours for a full refund.

How are products kept fresh?

All our products are sourced directly from farms and stored in temperature-controlled conditions to ensure maximum freshness.
